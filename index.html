Rice pudding

Posted at: March 13, 2015

Ingredients

  • 1 liter of milk
  • 1 packet of sugar or vanilla sugar
  • 250g of rice pudding (half the package)
  • 1 jar of cherries

Possible additions

  • 1 can of peaches (instead of cherries)
  • Cinnamon

Preparation

  1. Cook milk
  2. Pour sugar and rice into hot milk
  3. Wait until milk is gone and stir every couple of minutes

Serve and eat

Put rice pudding in a bowl, season with cinnamon and put cold cherries on top.

Noodles, Pesto and Olives

Posted at: March 13, 2015

Ingredients

  • Salt
  • 1 bag or glass of green olives
  • 1 glass of green pesto genoves
  • 100-200g of noodles (~half a package)

Preparation

  1. Put salt in water and boil it
  2. Cook noodles
  3. Cut olives
  4. When ready, let the water out of the pot
  5. Add a quarter of the pesto glass and olives

Serve and eat

Stir a bit and it's ready to eat

Sommersalat

Posted at: March 13, 2015

Ingredients

  • Salt, Pepper, Herbs
  • 1 Edam, Cheddar or Gouda cheese
  • 1 Cucumber
  • A bunch of tomatoes
  • 1 Avocado
  • 1 Bologna sausage or ham pieces
  • 1 Baguette

Possible additions

  • 1 Mozarella
  • 1 Paprika/Pepper/Capsicum
  • 1 Red onion
  • A pinch of garlic powder

Preparation

  1. Cut everything in little pieces
  2. Season everything with salt, pepper and herbs
  3. Cut the bread

Serve and eat

Mix everything in a big bowl or cooking pot.
